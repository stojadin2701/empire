Description

Offering modern luxury apartments in the heart of Sydney central business district (central business district), Meriton Serviced Apartments Kent Street boasts an indoor swimming pool, a fitness center, sauna and hot tub. Many suites offer excellent views of the city. Guests enjoy 1 GB of free WiFi per 24 hours.

The air-conditioned suites feature laundry facilities. Each suite has a sitting area with flat-screen cable TV and a DVD player. Studios have a kitchenette, and all other suites have a full kitchen.

The apartment complex is located across from the trendy World Square shopping center, 2 minutes’ walk from Town Hall Station. Queen Victoria Building and Darling Harbour are each within a 10-minute walk.

Sydney Central Business District is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, food and sightseeing.

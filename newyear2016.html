Sydney
The City of Bridges

Sydney Harbour is the place to be on New Year's Eve. Celebrate in style at some of Sydney's best hotels. Many of the city's hotels get into the spirit with festive New Year's Eve events, special meals and accommodation packages. Be sure to book early for your New Year's and summer holidays so you don't miss out on any of the fun!

Rio de Janeiro
The City of God

The most festive New Year‘s Celebration in the world happens in Rio de Janeiro. Reveillon in Copacabana is a wonderful way to start fresh. Two million Cariocas and visitors, many dressed in white for good luck, gather at the horseshoe-shaped beach. Stages with live music shows keep everyone busy until the countdown to midnight. Get ready to be dazzled with an amazing fireworks festival. Sharing the moment with so many people is a life-changing experience.

Description

Just 247 m from Nice-Ville Train Station, Hôtel des Flandres is a 15-minute walk from the beach and the Promenade des Anglais, and a 20-minute walk from Nice Old Town. It features free Wi-Fi throughout and a 24-hour front desk with luggage storage.

The hotel rooms have been individually decorated and include a TV with international channels. The private bathroom has a bathtub or shower. A computer is at guests' disposal in the lounge.

The hotel serves an all-you-can-eat buffet breakfast every morning, which can be enjoyed in the breakfast room or in the comfort of guest rooms. Many groceries, bars and restaurants can be found within a 15-minute walk.

The bus stops, with services on lines 98 and 99 for Nice Côte d’Azur Airport, are a 5-minute walk away. Guests can receive a discount rate for the Thiers parking lot, which is also a 5-minute walk from the property.

This is our guests' favorite part of Nice, according to independent reviews.

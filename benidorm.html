Description

Near Copacabana Beach and featuring scenic views of Christ the Redeemer and the Sugarloaf, this hotel provides a rooftop pool, gourmet restaurant and gym. WiFi is free. Parking is available at an additional price.

Bright and spacious, the air-conditioned rooms at Benidorm Palace display local paintings and framed artwork. They all offer cable TV and a well-supplied minibar, while suites also feature a private sauna and hot tub.

Guests can enjoy continental breakfast or a rich tropical buffet breakfast in Benidorm Palace´s stylish restaurant. Brazilian and international specialities are in the menu, which includes snacks and exotic drinks as well.

Siqueira Campos Metro Station is 550 m away. Copacabana Fort is 2.4 km from Benidorm Palace Hotel. Santos Dumont Airport is 10 km from the property and Galeão International Airport is at a distance of 22.6 km.

Copacabana is a great choice for travellers interested in beach walks, sightseeing and sandy beaches.

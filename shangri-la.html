Description

Situated in Bangalore, the garden city of India, the Shangri-La Hotel features an award winning restaurant and 5 speciality alfresco dining options, an outdoor swimming pool and a well-equipped health club.

The elegantly appointed guest rooms include private bathrooms featuring walk-in wardrobes and rain showers, luxurious amenities, bathrobes, and a hairdryer. Daily housekeeping is included, satellite TV channels, a spacious writing desk and complimentary high-speed internet access.

The hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and express check-in and check-out services, a 24-hour gymnasium, the exclusive CHI, The Spa, car hire, a business centre and the largest banqueting and meeting facilities in Bengaluru.

Dining options include Mediterranean, Indian, Japanese and Chinese speciality restaurants, outdoor bars and the cosmopolitan b Café – a culinary theatre of international delights.

The Shangri-La Hotel Bengaluru is 8 km from both the City Railway Station and Majestic Bus Station while the Kempegowda International Airport is 32 km away. The famous M. Chinnaswamy Stadium is located within 2 km from the property.

Palazzo Dama

Offering an outdoor pool and a garden, Palazzo Dama is set in the Spagna district in Rome, a 3-minute walk from Piazza del Popolo. The hotel has a lounge bar, restaurant and rooms combining contemporary décor with vintage design objects.

Hotel Raphael

One street from Piazza Navona, Hotel Raphael offers luxurious rooms with marble bathroom and parquet floors. Its roof terrace boasts panoramic views of Rome, from St. Peter's to the Pantheon.
